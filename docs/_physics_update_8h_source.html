217 Physics runs in its own phase, after AI, but before all others.
218 It's actually quite important that AI (the thing that drives Locomotors) and Physics
219 run in the same order, relative to each other, for a given object; otherwise,
220 interesting oscillations can occur in some situations, with friction being applied
221 either before or after the locomotive force, making for huge stuttery messes. (srj)
