39// Created: Colin Day, June 2001

41// Desc: Helpful interface for Sliders
42//
43// Slider Sliders are constructed of 4 pieces, two end pieces,
44// a repeating center, and a smaller repeating center pieces
45// to fill small seams. Since there are vertical sliders
46// and horizontal sliders, the LEFT end of a horizontal
47// slider is stored in the same place as the TOP end for a
48// vertical slider. The RIGHT end of a horizontal is
49// also equivalent to the BOTTOM end of a vertical one.
50//
51//-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
