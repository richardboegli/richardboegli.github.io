22This is just a couple of convenience functions for determining what timezone
23we are now in. It even accounts for daylight savings! One caveat is that it
24only tells you info about what the daylight savings info is now, not 5 minutes
25from now, not 2 hours ago. Oh well.
26\****************************************************************************/
